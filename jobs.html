About us

Ghosts is an independent team of artists making games based in Reykjavik, Iceland. We work remotely with team members in Norway, Belgium, The Netherlands and UK. It was founded by the 2 main developers behind the game NUTS (2021) to make a unique racing game. We are committed to creating a fair and safe workspace, collaborating with experienced artists, with support and flexibility for the workers.

Our next game is being developed in Unity for PC and consoles. It is a time trial racing game with a narrative single player experience and an online leaderboard component.

We are currently looking for several collaborators for contracts from now until roughly summer 2023. We are ideally looking for experienced candidates within Europe-compatible timezones. We are open and flexible to people working remotely and part-time or half-time.
